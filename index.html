Johnathan's Portfolio

This will be my personal portfolio website. Having a website that will display all my projects and ideas in an organized fashion will help me be able to document their progress. This website will include professional and personal information about me and provide in-depth information about how these projects were created. The end goal is to create a page that I can eventually make revenue off of to sustain my way of life while also developing a community of learning and inspiration.
